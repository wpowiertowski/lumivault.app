iCloud Catalog Sync

LumiVault can store its catalog in iCloud Drive, keeping your album metadata synchronized across all your Macs.

Enabling iCloud Sync

  1. Open Settings > iCloud (⌘,).
  2. Enable iCloud catalog sync.
  3. LumiVault moves the catalog to your iCloud Drive container.

What Syncs

  • Catalog metadata — album names, dates, file lists, SHA-256 hashes, storage locations.
  • Settings — volume bookmarks are device-specific and do not sync.

What Does Not Sync

  • Photo files — actual image data stays on your external volumes and B2. Only the catalog (metadata) syncs.
  • Thumbnails — regenerated locally from available volumes.
  • Volume bookmarks — each Mac manages its own volume access.

Conflict Resolution

If the catalog is modified on two devices simultaneously, iCloud Drive's file-level conflict resolution applies. LumiVault will detect the conflict on next launch and prompt you to choose which version to keep.

Tip: iCloud sync is for catalog metadata only. Your photos remain safely stored on external volumes and B2 regardless of iCloud status.
