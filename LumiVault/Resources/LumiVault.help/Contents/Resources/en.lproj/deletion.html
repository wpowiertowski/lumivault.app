Deleting Albums & Photos

LumiVault can remove photos and albums from the catalog, external volumes, and B2 in one step.

Deleting an Album

  1. Right-click (or Control-click) an album in the sidebar.
  2. Select Delete Album from the context menu.
  3. Confirm the deletion in the alert dialog.

This removes:

  • The album entry and all its photo records from the catalog.
  • All associated files from every configured external volume.
  • All associated files from B2 (if B2 is enabled).
  • PAR2 parity files alongside the originals.

Deleting Individual Photos

  1. Select a photo in the grid.
  2. Right-click and select Delete Photo, or press ⌫ (Delete).
  3. Confirm the deletion.

The photo is removed from the album, all volumes, and B2.

What Deletion Does Not Affect

  • Your Photos library — LumiVault never modifies Apple Photos. Deleting from LumiVault only removes the archived copies.
  • Other albums — if the same image (by SHA-256) exists in another album, those references are unaffected.
Warning: Deletion is permanent. Files are removed from all storage locations. Make sure you have the originals in Photos or another backup before deleting.
